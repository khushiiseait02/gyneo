ABOUT GYNECARE

GyneCare is an AI-powered platform that helps women monitor their reproductive health. It focuses on early detection and tracking of conditions like PCOS and Ovarian Cancer. Users can track cycles, check symptoms, upload scans for AI-based analysis, and assess genetic risks. The system also provides personalized insights and a health chatbot for instant guidance.
